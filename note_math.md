$$ \int_0 ^\infty \frac{1}{1 + x^2} \frac{1}{1 + x^N} dx $$

$$\begin{aligned}
  I &=& \int_0^\infty \frac{u^N}{(1+u^2)(1+u^N)} du \\
  &=& \int_0^\infty \frac{1 - 1 +u^N}{(1+u^2)(1+u^N)} du \\
  &=& - I + \int_0^\infty \frac{1}{(1+u^2)} du \\
  &=& - I + \frac{\pi}{2}
\end{aligned}$$
